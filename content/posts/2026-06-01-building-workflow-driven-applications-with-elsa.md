# Building Workflow-Driven Applications with Elsa

Elsa is a workflow engine for .NET applications that lets teams model, execute, and manage long-running business processes.

## Why workflows?

Many applications eventually need to coordinate steps, decisions, integrations, timers, approvals, and retries.

Elsa gives these processes a dedicated runtime and visual model instead of spreading orchestration logic across services, controllers, and background jobs.
